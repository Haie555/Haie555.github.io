About Me

I am a Senior Lecturer at the MSU-BIT-SMBU Joint Research Center of Applied Mathematics, Shenzhen MSU-BIT University. My research focuses on inverse problems, regularization methods, and stochastic algorithms with applications in various scientific domains.
